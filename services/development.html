03 — Development

Boutique Development

Creating experiential commercial environments across Arizona that drive lasting value for investors, tenants, and communities.

Our Role
Vidl's development practice focuses on boutique commercial projects that transform underperforming assets into curated experiential destinations. As both General Partner and Limited Partner across select Arizona developments, we bring a unique combination of brokerage intelligence, brand strategy expertise, and capital markets relationships to every project. Our developments prioritize design excellence, strategic tenant curation, and long-term community impact across Phoenix, Scottsdale, and Arizona's high-growth corridors.
01

Strategic Site Acquisition

We identify development opportunities across the Greater Phoenix metropolitan area before they reach the broader market — targeting infill locations, emerging corridors, and repositioning candidates in Scottsdale, Phoenix, Tempe, and the East Valley.

02

Experiential Tenant Curation

Every Vidl development is anchored by a carefully curated tenant mix designed to create a cohesive experiential environment. We leverage our brokerage relationships to attract high-caliber brands that drive foot traffic and elevate the overall asset.

03

Design-Forward Execution

We collaborate with top Arizona architects and designers to create environments that align closer to Architectural Digest than a traditional retail center. Clean lines, intentional materiality, and spaces that tell a story — because design drives both lease rates and consumer engagement.
