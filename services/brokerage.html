01 — Brokerage

Full-Service Brokerage

Strategic tenant representation and landlord advisory across Arizona's most competitive commercial real estate markets.

What We Do
Vidl's brokerage practice goes beyond traditional transaction management. We combine deep Phoenix metropolitan market intelligence with brand-level strategic thinking to place tenants in high-performing locations — from Scottsdale's luxury corridors to Downtown Phoenix's emerging urban retail nodes. Every deal is structured to compound brand equity, optimize lease economics, and position our clients for long-term market dominance.
01

Strategic Tenant Representation

We represent national and regional brands seeking premier Arizona locations. Our site selection process maps foot traffic, co-tenancy dynamics, and demographic alignment across Greater Phoenix to identify opportunities that drive revenue and amplify brand presence.

02

Landlord & Developer Advisory

We advise developers and property owners on tenant mix strategy, lease structuring, and asset positioning — transforming retail centers across Scottsdale, Phoenix, and the East Valley into curated experiential destinations that command premium rents.

03

Co-Investment & Portfolio Strategy

For select opportunities, Vidl co-invests alongside partners to align incentives and create shared value. Our portfolio strategy identifies undervalued assets and emerging corridors across Arizona's commercial real estate landscape.
