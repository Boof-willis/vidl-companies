Our Team

The people behind the platform.

Andrew LaPorte

Founder & CEO

Andrew LaPorte is the Founder and CEO of Vidl Companies, a real estate advisory and development platform focused on experiential brands, strategic growth, and high-impact placements across premier markets.

He has built a reputation as a trusted operator and advisor to founders, developers, and brands seeking to scale with intention. Over the course of his career, Andrew has been involved in nearly $1 billion in transactional volume, representing and curating tenancy across high-profile assets nationwide while helping brands expand through strategic real estate execution.

Through Vidl, Andrew leads initiatives across brokerage, boutique development, project strategy, and brand advisory, working at the intersection of real estate, business growth, and experiential retail. His approach is highly selective, focused on aligning the right brands, locations, and partnerships to create long-term value.

In addition to Vidl, Andrew is a co-founder of Sculp, a category-defining body sculpting concept based in Old Town Scottsdale, where he oversees growth strategy, operations, and brand positioning alongside his partner, Jaini. He is also actively involved in multiple ventures spanning wellness, development, and brand expansion, all centered around building scalable, experience-driven businesses.

Andrew is known for his ability to identify opportunity, structure deals, and bring the right people together to execute at a high level. He leads with discipline, clarity, and a strong focus on performance, while maintaining a deep commitment to the relationships that drive long-term success.

Based in Arizona, Andrew values family, faith, and living with intention. Outside of business, he is deeply connected to the motorsports community and spends his time driving and building within the enthusiast space.

Jaini Doffing

Partner & Chief Strategy Officer

Jaini Doffing serves as a Partner and Chief Strategy Officer at Vidl Companies, where she plays a key role in shaping the firm's strategic direction, partnerships, and client alignment.

She is actively involved in evaluating business opportunities, refining deal strategy, and works directly alongside executive leadership to position Vidl for long-term growth across its portfolio. Jaini brings a strong focus on identifying and aligning with high-quality brands, helping guide client relationships that fit Vidl's vision for scalable, experience-driven real estate.

Her involvement spans strategic planning, brand alignment, and business development, ensuring that each engagement is intentional, well-positioned, and built for growth.
